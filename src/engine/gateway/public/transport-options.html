What Changes The Answer

Trip Length

A 3- or 4-day trip usually pushes the recommendation toward flying. Longer open-ended travel makes driving more reasonable.

Arrival Energy

If you want to arrive ready to move, explore, or meet people, airfare typically preserves the most energy.

On-the-ground Flexibility

If you want spontaneous detours, extra gear, or to string together multiple Colorado stops, driving gains value.

How Monday Would Say It
Recommendation

If your goal is to take a practical Denver trip, I would recommend airfare. It is the best tradeoff because it protects time and lets the destination hold the weight of the experience instead of the transit.

Alternate Case

I would shift toward driving only if you want the road to be part of the memory, or if you expect the car itself to unlock a looser multi-stop Colorado trip.
